The family secret

They say coffee brings people together. We believe it brings families together too. La Corenita is not just a coffee brand; it's the result of a legacy that sprouted in different lands, yet with the same aroma of tradition, effort, and love.

It all started with a cup. The one served in Grandma Hortensia's kitchen, with a strong aroma, sweet notes, and that flavor that awakens your senses… and your memories.

On the Caribbean coast, in Sincelejo, Grandpa Corena—yes, the one everyone affectionately called “el viejo Core”—used to make his coffee with a devotion that felt from another world. He didn't need many words, but after every cup, he'd smile and say: “Now that I’ve had my coffee, let anything come my way.”

… Meanwhile, in the mountains of El Peñol, Antioquia, little Enrique—the father of Father Ricardo—was growing coffee like someone guarding a treasure.

Destiny, or perhaps coffee itself, brought these stories together. That’s how La Corenita was born: a brand that honors the memory of old Core and the dream of Antioquian coffee growers.

Today, when you taste a cup of La Corenita, you’re sipping the story of a family that chose to stay true to its roots, honor their elders, and share with you the secret that used to be passed down only from generation to generation.

Welcome to our story. Sit down, pour yourself a cup, and join us. You’re home ☕

What makes La Corenita special?

  • ☕ Origin: El Peñol, Antioquia – pesticide-free farm
  • 🔥 Roast: Medium, with an outstanding aroma
  • 🍫 Flavor: Smooth, lasting, with notes of chocolate and panela
  • 💛 Production: Handcrafted in small batches
  • 📦 Sizes: 250g and 500g
