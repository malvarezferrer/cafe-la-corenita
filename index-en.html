The Family Secret

They say coffee brings people together. We believe it brings families together too. La Corenita isn’t just a coffee brand—it’s a legacy that sprouted in different lands, with the same aroma of tradition, effort and love.

It all started with a cup. The one served in grandma Hortensia’s kitchen, with a strong aroma, sweet notes, and that flavor that awakens your senses... and your memories.

On the Caribbean coast, in Sincelejo, grandpa Corena—yes, the one everyone fondly called “el viejo Core”—used to brew his coffee with devotion that seemed from another world. He didn’t say much, but every time he made a cup, he’d look at us and say: “I’ve had my coffee, now bring it on.”

... Meanwhile, in the mountains of El Peñol, Antioquia, Enriquito—the father of father Ricardo—was growing coffee with the care of someone protecting a treasure...

Destiny, or maybe coffee itself, brought those stories together. That’s how La Corenita was born: a brand that honors grandpa Core’s memory and the dream of Antioquian coffee growers.

Today, when you sip a cup of La Corenita... you’re drinking the story of a family that chose to preserve its roots, honor its elders, and share with you a secret once passed only from generation to generation.

Welcome to our story. Sit down, pour yourself a cup, and join us. You’re home ☕

What Makes La Corenita Special?

  • ☕ Origin: El Peñol, Antioquia – grown without pesticides
  • 🔥 Roast: Medium, with a standout aroma
  • 🍫 Flavor: Smooth, persistent, with notes of chocolate and panela
  • 💛 Handmade production in small batches
  • 📦 Available sizes: 250g and 500g
