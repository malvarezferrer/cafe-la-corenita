The family secret

It all began with a hug between mountains and Caribbean breezes. A family from El Peñol and one from Sincelejo came together not only through love, but a shared legacy: coffee.

Café La Corenita is born from that union. It's the flavor of the land, the work of hands that believe in craftsmanship, and the connection between two worlds that found harmony in every lovingly roasted bean.

What started as a family dream is now shared with pride in every cup. Because this isn't just coffee. It's a living story — roasted, smelled, and savored.

What makes La Corenita special?

  • ☕ Origin: El Peñol, Antioquia – insecticide-free farm
  • 🔥 Roast: Medium, with outstanding aroma
  • 🍫 Taste: Smooth, lasting, with notes of chocolate and panela
  • 💛 Artisanal production in small batches
  • 📦 Presentations: 250g and 500g
